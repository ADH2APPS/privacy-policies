Privacy Policy - ICS Viewer

TL;DR: ICS Viewer is completely offline. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your calendar files stay on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

ICS Viewer is designed as a completely offline utility. The app only:

  • Reads ICS calendar files you explicitly open
  • Stores basic app preferences locally (display settings, recurring event limits)
  • Does not access your personal calendars, contacts, or any other data

2. How We Use Information

The app processes ICS calendar files entirely on your device to:

  • Display calendar events from the ICS file you opened
  • Enable search and filtering within those events
  • Remember your display preferences (recurring events settings, day start time)

No data is transmitted anywhere. Everything happens locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

ICS Viewer stores only these technical preferences locally on your device:

  • Whether to show recurring events (true/false)
  • Maximum number of recurring instances to display (number)
  • Day start time preferences (hour/minute)

These are stored in Android's SharedPreferences and contain no personal information.

4. Permissions

The app requests these permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below) - To read ICS files you choose to open
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+) - For file picker functionality
  • MANAGE_DOCUMENTS - To use Android's file picker for selecting ICS files

These permissions are used solely to let you choose and open ICS calendar files.

5. Third-Party Services

None. ICS Viewer uses no third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising networks.

6. Data Sharing

We don't share any data because we don't collect any. The app works completely offline.

7. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored remotely, your calendar information remains secure on your device. The app only processes the specific ICS files you choose to open.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages as it collects no personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page. Last updated: January 2025.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the Google Play Store listing for ICS Viewer.
